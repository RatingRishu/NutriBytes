Nutrition during menopause

A good variety of protein is not only important but tends to keep you fuller for longer, so ensure you incorporate protein into every meal and snack if you can. When choosing protein foods think about trying to include at least one portion of oily fish into your diet per week (salmon, trout, fresh tuna, mackerel).
